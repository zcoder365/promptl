About Promptl 💡

Making writing fun for every kind of writer

Why Promptl?

I'm passionate about creative writing, but I understand that not everyone feels the same way. I was inspired to develop Promptl by my younger sister, who has dyslexia. As a result of this learning difference, she saw writing as a chore. I created Promptl to help kids like her develop writing skills in a fun and rewarding way.

How does it work?

Promptl generates five random words for use in a short story: a character, an occupation, a thing, a place, and a bonus word (worth twice as much as the others).

To encourage thoughtful writing, you'll earn points by writing at least 70 words and using the prompt words. Surpass 100 words and you'll unlock a 25-point bonus on top of your prompt rewards! 🎉
