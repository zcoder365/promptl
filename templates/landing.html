Common questions

Is Promptl free?

Yes! Promptl is completely free to use. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no ads.

Who is Promptl for?

Promptl is designed for kids with learning differences like dyslexia who find writing intimidating — but it works just as well for any kid (or adult!) who wants to make writing more fun. There's no minimum or maximum age.

How is this different from regular writing prompts?

Most writing prompts give you a starting sentence or topic. Promptl gives you 5 random words and lets you build the story however you want — which lowers the pressure to come up with something "perfect" and makes it feel more like play.

Is my child's data safe?

Stories are stored privately and only visible to the account that wrote them. No data is sold to third parties, and stories are never shared publicly. Promptl uses Firebase Authentication (the same tech that powers many major apps) to keep accounts secure.

Will there be a teacher version?

A teacher dashboard is in development! It will let educators assign writing prompts and view their students' submissions. If you're a teacher interested in early access, reach out below.

Who built Promptl?

Promptl was built by Zoe Droulias, a McGill University Computer Science student, originally inspired by her younger sister who has dyslexia and saw writing as a chore.
